# Handheld Prototype Notes

## Concept

Portable embedded engineering and diagnostics platform inspired by:

- aviation field equipment
- industrial service tools
- cyberpunk interfaces
- sci-fi AI terminals

## Current Mechanical Ideas

### Prototype Materials

- PTFE cover plates
- aluminum standoffs
- 3D printed enclosure sections
- temporary perfboard mounting

### Form Factor Direction

- approximately phone-width handheld
- physical navigation buttons
- integrated display
- side-mounted connectors
- modular sensor or communications expansion

## Current UI Hardware

- LilyGo T-Display P4
- OLED display modules
- small momentary pushbuttons
- status LEDs

## Mechanical Priorities

1. Comfortable one-hand operation
2. Accessible USB and debug ports
3. Internal mounting points
4. Battery compartment planning
5. Heat management
6. Serviceability

## Future Concepts

- detachable side modules
- fold-out diagnostics interface
- drone field programming dock
- ruggedized enclosure version
- shoulder or belt holster integration

## Design Philosophy

The device should feel like professional equipment, not a toy dev board in a plastic box.
